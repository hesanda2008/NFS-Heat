Need for Speed Heat (stylised as NFS Heat) is a 2019 racing video game developed
by Ghost Games and published by Electronic Arts for PlayStation 4, Windows, and
Xbox One. It is the twenty-fourth installment in the Need for Speed series
and commemorates the series' 25th anniversary.
